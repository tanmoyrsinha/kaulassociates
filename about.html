Our Story

A Decade of Proven Results

Kaul & Associates is a Bangalore-based management consulting firm specialising in the transformation of manufacturing operations. Founded by Vinay Kaul, the firm brings together a team of practitioners who have spent their careers inside India's most demanding industrial environments.

Over more than a decade, we have assisted companies across aerospace, consumer electronics, energy, automotive components, textiles, and capital goods — helping them build the capabilities needed to compete in a rapidly changing global marketplace.

Our approach is grounded in the belief that lasting improvement requires transferring real expertise to client teams, not just delivering reports. We work alongside your people from policy deployment through project execution, ensuring the gains are embedded and self-sustaining.

🎯 Our Approach

  • Understand each client's distinct context and constraints
  • Design custom solutions — no one-size-fits-all templates
  • Work side-by-side with client teams during execution
  • Build cross-functional performance measures for sustained improvement
  • Transfer knowledge so clients become self-sufficient

📈 Why We Are Different

  • Consultants with decades of industry experience
  • Deep domain expertise across complementary disciplines
  • Visiting faculty at IIM Ahmedabad, IIM Bangalore, IIM Udaipur, and IIM Nagpur
