Our Story

A Decade of Proven Results

Kaul & Associates is a Bangalore-based management consulting firm specialising in the transformation of manufacturing operations. Founded by Vinay Kaul, the firm brings together a team of practitioners who have spent their careers inside India's most demanding industrial environments.

Over more than a decade, we have assisted companies across aerospace, consumer electronics, energy, automotive components, textiles, and capital goods — helping them build the capabilities needed to compete in a rapidly changing global marketplace.

Our approach is grounded in the belief that lasting improvement requires transferring real expertise to client teams, not just delivering reports. We work alongside your people from policy deployment through project execution, ensuring the gains are embedded and self-sustaining.

🎯 Our Approach

  • Understand each client's distinct context and constraints
  • Design custom solutions — no one-size-fits-all templates
  • Work side-by-side with client teams during execution
  • Build cross-functional performance measures for sustained improvement
  • Transfer knowledge so clients become self-sufficient

📈 Why We Are Different

Four Decades of Excellence

Milestones & Achievements

From IIT Bombay to the shopfloors of India's most demanding manufacturers — four decades of expertise that now comes to your organisation.

Late 1980s Education

IIT Bombay — Mechanical & Machine Tool Technology

Vinay Kaul graduates with Graduate and Post-Graduate degrees in Mechanical & Machine Tool Technology from IIT Bombay — the foundation of an industrial career spanning four decades and every major sector of Indian manufacturing.

1990s Career

Enter India's Industrial Manufacturing Sector

Joins the shopfloor world of Indian manufacturing — building hands-on expertise in production engineering, plant operations, and quality systems across the country's most demanding industrial environments.

Late 1990s Achievement

Executive Vice President — Mysore Kirloskar Ltd

Appointed Executive Vice President at Mysore Kirloskar Ltd, one of India's most respected engineering companies. Responsible for large-scale manufacturing operations, plant performance, and the operational discipline that would later define the consulting practice.

2000s Academic

Visiting Faculty — IIM Ahmedabad

Designs and delivers the Operations Excellence programme at IIM Ahmedabad — bringing real shopfloor experience into India's premier management institution. The appointment is later extended to IIM Bangalore, IIM Udaipur, and IIM Nagpur, reaching hundreds of future business leaders annually.

c. 2012 Founding

Kaul & Associates — Established

The consulting practice is founded in Bangalore, bringing together a team of industrial practitioners dedicated to transforming manufacturing operations. The firm's mandate: transfer genuine expertise to client teams, not just deliver reports.

2013–15 Achievement

BPL Group & Videocon — Lean & Supply Chain Transformation

Major programmes across India's largest consumer electronics companies. ₹250 crore freed from BPL's warehouse inventory. Videocon's output doubled to 10,000 units per day from the same line and same workforce. Product development lead time at BPL cut from 14 months to 7.

2014–16 Achievement

Hindustan Aeronautics Limited — Aerospace Lean

Cellular manufacturing implemented at HAL's Engine Division, Bangalore. Bottleneck output doubled. The engagement brings Lean thinking — including operator cross-training and Kaizen workshops — to one of the most demanding quality environments in Indian industry.

2016 ★ Recognition

Boeing Endorsement — Tata Advanced Materials

The plant layout designed by Kaul & Associates for Tata Advanced Materials is recognised by Boeing executives as "one of the finest layouts we have seen anywhere in the world." A global endorsement from the world's largest aerospace company.

2018–22 ★ Recognition

Best TPM Globally — Voith Group

Following a four-year cultural and operational transformation at Voith Turbo, Hyderabad, the TPM implementation is independently rated the best among all Voith group companies worldwide. Sales grew from ₹75Cr to ₹205Cr. EBITDA reached 37% of sales. ROCE 63%.

2020–24 Achievement

Capital Equipment Cluster — Sulzer, Burckhardt, Tranter

Revenue tripled at Sulzer Pumps (₹90Cr → ₹270Cr). Output doubled at Sulzer Chemtech. On-time delivery lifted to 95% at Burckhardt Compression and Tranter India. Self-managed teams established at Burckhardt — "bringing out the spirit of teamwork and ownership."

2024 Team

Aarti Ganesh Joins as Senior Consultant

The team expands with Aarti Ganesh — Mechanical Engineer with a Master's from UT Austin and an ISB Management Diploma. 19+ years of expertise in Lean Manufacturing, Plant Layout Design, and Operational Excellence, with a consistent track record of cutting lead times by 25% and lifting on-time delivery above 95%.

2025–26 Milestone

17 Manufacturers Transformed

Kaul & Associates reaches its 17th major engagement — spanning aerospace, consumer electronics, energy, capital equipment, and industrial products across India. The firm continues to take on assignments where the challenge is not just operational, but cultural.
