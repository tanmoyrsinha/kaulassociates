Domain 02

Six Sigma

Six Sigma provides the statistical rigour to reduce process variation and eliminate defects permanently.

DMAIC Methodology

Our Black Belt programmes and consulting engagements follow the proven DMAIC framework (6–9 months for full Black Belt certification):

  • Define — Voice of Customer and project baselining
  • Measure — Process mapping and systems thinking
  • Analyse — Root cause identification and FMEA
  • Improve — Design of Experiments (DOE)
  • Control — Process capability, SPC, and control plans

Six Sigma Tools We Deploy

  • Voice of Customer (VoC) analysis
  • Process capability studies (Cp, Cpk)
  • Failure Mode & Effects Analysis (FMEA)
  • Statistical Process Control (SPC)
  • Design of Experiments (DOE)
  • Design for Manufacturability (DFM)
  • Measurement System Analysis (MSA / Gauge R&R)
  • Black Belt training and mentoring programmes

Client: Triveni Engineering & Industries

Applied Six Sigma methodology to drive significant quality improvements in the manufacture of steam and gas turbines at facilities in Bangalore and Mysore.
