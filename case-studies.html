Lean Manufacturing Aerospace

Cellular Manufacturing — HAL Engine Division

Client: Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL), Engine Division, Bangalore
Product: Aero-engines

HAL's Engine Division was producing critical aero-engine components in a traditional job-shop layout, resulting in long lead times, high work-in-progress inventory, and limited visibility of flow. Kaul & Associates designed and implemented a cellular manufacturing arrangement, grouping machines and people around component families. The new cells dramatically shortened the physical distance materials travelled, reduced queue times between operations, and created accountability for quality at each stage of production. Training and Kaizen workshops ensured the workforce understood and owned the new way of working.

Key Interventions

  • Component family analysis
  • Cell layout design
  • Flow path optimisation
  • Operator cross-training
  • Kaizen workshops
Lean Manufacturing Energy

Factory Layout Redesign — Tata BP Solar

Client: Tata BP Solar, Bangalore
Product: Solar Panels

Tata BP Solar's existing plant layout had evolved organically and created significant material handling waste, long travel distances, and production bottlenecks. Kaul & Associates conducted a systematic analysis of material flows and value streams, then designed a revised layout guided by Lean flow principles. The new arrangement aligned the physical flow of materials with the sequence of manufacturing operations, reduced handling effort, improved space utilisation, and created clearer visual management of production status across the facility.

Key Interventions

  • Value stream mapping
  • Material flow analysis
  • Layout simulation
  • Visual factory design
  • Implementation support

Lean Manufacturing Capital Equipment

Master Plant layout — Sulzer Pumps

Client: Sulzer Pumps, Mumbai
Products: High performance pumps

Implemented a suite of Lean interventions — Make to Order flow, Master Plant Layout with Cellular Design, and a Pull System with Heijunka — to transform manufacturing operations for high-performance pumps. The programme tripled output from the same facility (₹90 Cr to ₹270 Cr), improved on-time delivery from 55% to 90%, and optimised resource utilisation while significantly reducing inventory levels.

Key Interventions

  • Lean – Make to Order
  • Master Plant Layout
  • Cellular Design
  • Pull System with Heijunka
Lean Manufacturing Fabrication

Plant layout and Planning system — Technocraft Industries

Client: Technocraft Industries, Murbad
Products: Scaffolding systems

Redesigned the plant layout and overhauled the production planning system for a scaffolding manufacturer operating under significant capacity and space constraints. The new layout eliminated congestion and non-value-added movement, while the planning system brought discipline and predictability to scheduling. Output tripled with no increase in manpower or floor space, and on-time delivery improved from 40% to 95%. A TPM programme is currently underway to sustain equipment reliability and build on these gains.

Key Interventions

  • New Plant Layout
  • Production Planning System
  • TPM (in progress)
Lean Manufacturing Industrial products

Lean Transformation — Flow Link Systems

Client: Flow Link Systems, Coimbatore
Products: Control Valves for Oil & Gas

Delivered a comprehensive Lean transformation for a precision valve manufacturer supplying the oil and gas sector. A redesigned plant layout and a new Lean Planning System together lifted output from ₹100 Cr to ₹155 Cr within twelve months and moved the business from breakeven to ₹12 Cr profit. SMED implementation cut CNC machine setup times from 60 minutes to 10 minutes, freeing significant capacity, while Lean Transport Solutions streamlined internal material handling across the facility. On-time delivery improved from 35% to 95%.

Key Interventions

  • Plant Layout Redesign
  • Lean Planning System
  • SMED
  • Lean Transport Solutions
